Smart Random Reminder Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Overview

Smart Random Reminder is designed to work locally on your Android device. The app stores your reminders and notification settings on your device so it can schedule reminders for you.

What data the app stores

  • Reminder text you create
  • Reminder schedule settings
  • Default notification hours

How the data is used

Your data is used only to display reminders in the app, schedule local notifications, and restore your reminders after device restart.

Data sharing

Smart Random Reminder does not send your reminder content or personal data to our servers. The app does not require account creation and does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking.

Permissions

The app requests notification permission to show reminders and boot permission to restore reminder scheduling after your device restarts or the app is updated.

Exports and backups

If you choose to export reminders, the app writes a text file to the location you select. That file is controlled by you and is not uploaded by the app.
