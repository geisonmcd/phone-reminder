Smart Random Reminder helps you keep short, intentional reminders on your phone without turning them into a complicated task manager.

Create reminders in seconds, choose how often they should appear, and keep the schedule inside the hours that work for you.

Features:
- Simple reminder list with fast editing
- Per-reminder frequency controls
- Up to 50 notifications per day for a single reminder
- Default notification hours for the whole app
- Case-insensitive reminder filtering
- Plain-text export and import
- Local storage on your device

Smart Random Reminder is designed for repeated prompts like:
- Focus cues
- Habit reminders
- Reflection prompts
- Personal rules
- Daily mindset checks

Privacy:
- No account required
- No cloud sync
- No analytics or ads
- Reminder data stays on your device unless you export it yourself
